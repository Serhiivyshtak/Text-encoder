Why actually Vyshtak encodeing system?

From time to time, we hear from the media about massive data braches. Modern life in the internet becomes more and more dangerous. As more technologies appears in plain sight, as clever scammers and thieves are. Vyshtak encoding system provides you functionality, which can be helpful if you want to feel safe about your information. Our encryption is impossible to decipher if you don't own the source code of this application. Paste your important text to the "Type your text to encode:" field and get already encoded text back. Then, if you want to decode your cipher, "Type your text to decode:" field stands for you.
